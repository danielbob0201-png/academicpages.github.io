### Synopsis

This work studies hallucination mitigation in LLMs from an inner representation perspective, introducing in-context sharpness as an alert signal for identifying factually inconsistent generations.

**Key contributions:**
- Novel inner-representation-based approach for hallucination detection
- In-context sharpness as an interpretable alert signal
- Published at ICML 2024
