### Synopsis

Despite strong performance on general VQA benchmarks, current vision-language models (VLMs) struggle significantly with chart-specific questions. This work identifies the key perception bottleneck arising from limited ability to recognize fine-grained visual elements in charts.

**Key contributions:**
- Systematic analysis of failure modes in VLM chart understanding
- Identification of the perception bottleneck in fine-grained visual element recognition
- Targeted interventions to improve chart reasoning accuracy
