### Synopsis

We discover and investigate a universal truthfulness hyperplane embedded within large language models -- a geometric structure that separates factual from hallucinatory representations in the model's internal space.

**Key contributions:**
- Discovery of a consistent geometric structure governing model truthfulness
- Cross-model validation showing universality across model families and scales
- Practical approach for predicting and mitigating model hallucinations
