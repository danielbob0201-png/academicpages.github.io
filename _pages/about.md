Hi, I'm **Junteng Liu** (鲜俊腾), a first-year PhD candidate in the NLP Group at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Junxian He. I completed my B.Eng. in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024.

My research sits at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning. I care about making large language models more capable, truthful, and interpretable. My current interests include:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning** -- improving how models reason step by step and learning from feedback
- **Hallucination & Truthfulness in VLMs** -- understanding and reducing false information in vision-language models
- **LLM Interpretability** -- probing the internal mechanisms of language models
