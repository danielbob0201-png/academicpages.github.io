# Hello, I'm Junteng Liu

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkust-nlp.github.io/), advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://jkjung.com/). I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024. Previously, I was advised by Prof. Junxian He during my undergraduate research at SJTU.

## Research Interests

My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning. In particular, I'm interested in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning** — Developing methods to improve reasoning capabilities in large language models and leveraging reinforcement learning for more reliable AI systems
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)** — Understanding and mitigating when VLMs generate factually inconsistent outputs, particularly in structured vision tasks such as chart understanding
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability** — Exploring internal mechanisms of LLMs that govern truthfulness and making black-box models more interpretable
