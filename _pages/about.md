## About Me

Hi, I'm **Junteng Liu** (刘俊腾) — a first-year PhD candidate in **Computer Science** at the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**, affiliated with the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkust-nlp.github.io) led by [Prof. Junxian He](https://hkust-nlp.github.io/jxhe/).

I completed my B.Eng. at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)** in June 2024. My research sits at the intersection of **Natural Language Processing** and **Machine Learning**, with a focus on making large language models more honest, interpretable, and capable of sound reasoning.

---

## Research Interests

- 🧠 **LLM Reasoning & Reinforcement Learning**
  - Developing data and training strategies to improve logical reasoning in large language models through reinforcement learning.

- 👁️ **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)**
  - Studying *why* VLMs produce incorrect or fabricated content and how to mitigate it.

- ✅ **LLM Truthfulness & Interpretability**
  - Investigating the internal representations of honesty and truthfulness in LLMs, and building interpretability tools to probe model behavior.
