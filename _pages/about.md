## About Me

I am a **first-year PhD candidate** at the **HKUST NLP Group** at the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**, under the supervision of **Professor Junxian He**. I graduated from **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)** in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree.

My research focuses on **natural language processing (NLP)** and **machine learning**, with specific interests in:
- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning** — improving how large language models perform logical and step-by-step reasoning.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)** — understanding and mitigating factually incorrect outputs in multi-modal systems.
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability** — probing the internal mechanisms of LLMs to understand and improve their reliability.
