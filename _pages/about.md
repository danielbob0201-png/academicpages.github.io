## About Me

I'm **Junteng Liu** (刘俊腾), a first-year PhD candidate in **Computer Science** at the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**, advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://hkust-nlp.github.io/jxhe/) in the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkust-nlp.github.io). I previously completed my B.Eng. at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)** in June 2024, where I was awarded the Zhiyuan Honor Scholarship.

---

## Research

My research explores how to make large language models **more truthful, interpretable, and capable of sound reasoning**. Current focus areas include:

- **LLM Reasoning & Reinforcement Learning** — training strategies and data synthesis to improve logical reasoning in LLMs.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)** — diagnosing why VLMs generate inaccurate or fabricated content and how to prevent it.
- **LLM Truthfulness & Interpretability** — probing the internal representations of honesty in LLMs and building tools to better understand model behavior.
