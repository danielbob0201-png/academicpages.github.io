**On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding**

Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He

[Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.05062) | [Code](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Vision4Chart)

## Abstract

Vision-Language Models (VLMs) show impressive chart understanding abilities, yet we identify and analyze a critical perception bottleneck that limits their performance on chart reasoning tasks. We investigate the root causes and propose targeted improvements.
