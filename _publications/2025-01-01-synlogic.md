**SynLogic: Synthesizing Verifiable Reasoning Data at Scale for Learning Logical Reasoning and Beyond**

Junteng Liu, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He

[Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.05062) | [Code](https://github.com/synlogic-project/synlogic)

## Abstract

We introduce SynLogic, a framework for synthesizing large-scale, verifiable reasoning datasets to effectively train language models on logical reasoning tasks. SynLogic generates diverse logical reasoning chains with ground-truth verifiable outcomes, enabling supervised fine-tuning that scales with task complexity.
