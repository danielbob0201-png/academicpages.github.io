**On the Universal Truthfulness Hyperplane Inside LLMs**

Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He

EMNLP 2024

[Paper](https://aclanthology.org/2024.emnlp-main.xxx/) | [Code](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane)

## Abstract

We discover a universal truthfulness hyperplane in the internal representation space of LLMs that organically separates truthful and untruthful responses across diverse tasks. By leveraging this geometric property, we propose an interpretable method for truthfulness alignment and provide empirical evidence of its effectiveness.
