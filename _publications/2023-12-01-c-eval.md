C-Eval is a comprehensive, multi-level, multi-discipline Chinese evaluation benchmark designed to assess the knowledge and reasoning capabilities of foundation models. It covers subjects from middle school to college entrance exams and professional certification exams, spanning 13 disciplines and four difficulty levels.
